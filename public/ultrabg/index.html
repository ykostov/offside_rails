About Us

Dry Cleaning ULTRA opened in 2008. In our workshops for professional dry cleaning and laundry services each customer receives high quality care. Our employees are highly experienced and the detergents that we use are with professional quality. Your laundry are thoroughly cleaned, ironed and packed. You can find us in Dianabad and Druzhba 2. For the convenience of clients we work with companies that offer professional dyeing clothes and carpet cleaning. If you do not have the time, we provide door-to-door delivery in convenient time and place. The minimum value of order is 12 lv. You can order delivery via online form or by phone 088 200 40 40. For corporate customers Dry Cleaning ULTRA offers long-term partnership at competitive prices.

Who Are We?

ULTRA Dry Cleaning opened in 2008. In our dry cleaning and professional laundry workshops, every customer receives high quality service. Our employees have many years of experience and the consumables we use are of high quality. Your clothes are carefully cleaned, ironed and packaged. You can find us in Dianabad and Druzba 2. To facilitate our clients we work with companies for professional dyeing of clothes and carpet washing. If you do not have the time, we offer delivery service at a convenient time and place for the customer. You can order delivery via the online form here or by phone on 088 200 40 40. For Corporate Clients Dry Cleaning ULTRA offers a long-term partnership at competitive prices.

Machines and Materials

In order to provide our customers with the necessary quality, we have equipped our studio with the latest generation of cleaning and washing machines from leading companies in the REALSTAR, DANUBE and SILK industries. They comply with all requirements of international environmental standards. In our work we use only high quality dry cleaning and laundry detergents from world-renowned manufacturers such as SEITZ, which guarantees the cleanliness, freshness and long-term use of your clothes, linen, home textiles and more.

Satisfied customers

At ULTRA, every customer receives a high quality service. Clothes are carefully cleaned, ironed and packaged. The deadlines for the fulfillment of the orders are respectively - for 24 and 72 hours, as well as express for 3 hours according to the individual requirements of each client.

Corporate clients

For Corporate Clients Dry Cleaning ULTRA offers a long-term partnership at competitive prices.
